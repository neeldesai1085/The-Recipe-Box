Spaghetti Bolognese

A hearty, rich, and comforting Italian pasta dish perfect for any occasion.

Recipe Information

Prep Time:
15 minutes
Cook Time:
45 minutes
Total Time:
1 hour
Servings:
4 servings
Difficulty:
Moderate

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g ground beef
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 400g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil and grated parmesan for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Saut√© onion and garlic until soft, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon.
  3. Stir in the carrot and celery, cooking for another 5 minutes.
  4. Pour in the chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, and red wine (if using). Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens.
  6. Meanwhile, cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  7. Serve the Bolognese sauce over the spaghetti. Garnish with fresh basil and parmesan.

Tips for Perfect Bolognese

  • Simmer Slowly: Letting the sauce cook slowly enhances flavor.
  • Use Fresh Herbs: Fresh basil adds a vibrant touch.
  • Wine Adds Depth: Red wine enriches the sauce's taste, but it's optional.
