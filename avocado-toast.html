Avocado Toast

Simple, healthy, and delicious avocado toast perfect for breakfast or a quick snack.

Recipe Information

Prep Time:
10 minutes
Cook Time:
5 minutes
Total Time:
15 minutes
Servings:
2
Difficulty:
Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 4 slices of bread (sourdough or whole grain preferred)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved (optional)
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Sesame seeds or everything bagel seasoning (optional)
  • Olive oil for drizzling (optional)

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden and crisp.
  2. Cut the avocados in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  3. Mash the avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper until smooth but slightly chunky.
  4. Spread the avocado mixture evenly on the toasted bread slices.
  5. Top with cherry tomatoes, red pepper flakes, sesame seeds, or any desired toppings.
  6. Drizzle with a little olive oil for extra flavor if desired.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tips for Perfect Avocado Toast

  • Choose ripe avocados: They should yield slightly to gentle pressure.
  • Use quality bread: Sourdough or whole grain works best for texture and flavor.
  • Customize toppings: Try poached eggs, feta cheese, or smoked salmon for variety.
  • Serve immediately: Avocado browns quickly, so enjoy fresh!
