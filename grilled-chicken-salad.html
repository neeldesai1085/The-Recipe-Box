Grilled Chicken Salad

A refreshing and nutritious salad with juicy grilled chicken, fresh greens, and a zesty dressing.

Recipe Information

Prep Time:
15 minutes
Cook Time:
10 minutes
Total Time:
25 minutes
Servings:
2
Difficulty:
Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cups mixed salad greens
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinaigrette dressing

Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush chicken breasts with olive oil and season with garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Grill chicken for 5-6 minutes per side until fully cooked. Let rest for 5 minutes, then slice.
  4. In a large bowl, combine salad greens, cherry tomatoes, red onion, cucumber, and feta cheese.
  5. Top with grilled chicken slices and drizzle with balsamic vinaigrette.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
